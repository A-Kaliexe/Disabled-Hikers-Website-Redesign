Storytelling Project

The Storytelling Project is more than just a community; it's a journey of words and tales.

Read the Stories of Disabled Hikers

The Storytelling Project was designed for all outdoor enthusiasts with disabilities. It is a chance to amplify your voice and share your unique experiences. We're on the lookout for individuals passionate about outdoor activities to contribute to our social media or blog. A stipend is provided, and we're always accepting applications.
